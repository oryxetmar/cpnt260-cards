Not All Plants Are The Same

Let's take a moment to match your lifestyle to the perfect plant. Some homes are humid and some can be quite dry. Some homes have lots of sunlight and some remain much darker. There are a lot of variables that we can take into account when setting ourselves and our plants up to thrive!
